# MerchNet Features Overview 🌟

Complete guide to all features and capabilities of MerchNet.

## 🏪 POS Billing System

### Quick Billing Interface
- **Visual Product Grid**: Browse products with images and prices
- **Instant Search**: Find products by name, SKU, or barcode
- **One-Click Add**: Click any product to add to cart
- **Real-time Cart**: See items, quantities, and totals instantly

### Cart Management
- **Quantity Control**: Increase/decrease with +/- buttons
- **Remove Items**: Quick delete from cart
- **Stock Validation**: Prevents overselling
- **Clear Cart**: Reset and start fresh

### Payment Processing
- **Multiple Methods**: Cash, Card, UPI support
- **Instant Calculation**: Auto-calculate subtotal, tax, total
- **Customer Selection**: Optional customer linking
- **Walk-in Sales**: No customer required

### Receipt Generation
- **Auto-Print**: Opens print dialog on sale completion
- **Professional Format**: Clean, readable receipt layout
- **Complete Details**: Items, quantities, prices, totals, tax
- **Business Info**: Your business name and details

## 📦 Product Management

### Product Catalog
- **Unlimited Products**: Add as many products as needed
- **Rich Information**: Name, description, SKU, barcode
- **Pricing**: Selling price and cost price tracking
- **Categories**: Organize products by category
- **Images**: Product image support (URL-based)

### Inventory Control
- **Stock Tracking**: Real-time stock levels
- **Auto-Updates**: Stock reduces on each sale
- **Low Stock Alerts**: Visual warnings for low inventory
- **Stock Movements**: Complete history of stock changes
- **Bulk Management**: Edit multiple products efficiently

### Product Features
- **SKU System**: Unique product identifiers
- **Barcode Support**: Scan or search by barcode
- **Active/Inactive**: Enable/disable products
- **Search & Filter**: Quick product lookup
- **Sorting**: Sort by name, price, stock, etc.
